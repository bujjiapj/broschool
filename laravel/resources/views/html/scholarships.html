Scholarships

The school offers scholarships for the meritorious students excelling in various fields.

Over all toppers in the class, state level achievers in sports, games, dance, oratorical, competitive exams or record holders like Limca Book of records, Asian Record

or Guinness record etc. are offered scholarships based on their performance and position.

Poor, orphan and deserving children are also considered after thorough scrutiny.
